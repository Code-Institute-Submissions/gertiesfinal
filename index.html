About us

Gerties Bakeshop and Fastfood is located in Cut cut 1, Tarlac City, Philippines. We started off selling pandesal baked early in the morning and sold in front of garage in 1990 and later on opened our first store. We expanded our shop and opened our 2nd store and the rest is histroy. We sell all day meals from breakfast, lunch and dinner. Visit our shop taste our famouse Baked Macaroni.

We have a range of pastries and customised cakes for different occassions.
